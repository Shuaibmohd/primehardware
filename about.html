About Us

Welcome to Prime Hardware Sanitary and Plumbing Solutions!


At Prime Hardware, we're more than just a plumbing supply store – we're your partners in creating functional and beautiful spaces. With a commitment to excellence and a passion for innovation, we provide a comprehensive range of high-quality plumbing and sanitary solutions to meet the diverse needs of our customers.


Our Story:

Prime Hardware has been a trusted name in the industry for over many years. Founded on the principles of integrity, reliability, and customer satisfaction, we have steadily grown to become a leading provider of plumbing and sanitary products in our region.


What We Offer:

From residential renovations to large-scale commercial projects, we offer a wide selection of products to suit every need and budget. Whether you're a homeowner, contractor, or designer, you'll find everything you need to complete your project with confidence.
